Signing In

  1. Like most websites, sign in can be found on the upper right. Hover over "Guest", then click SIGN IN.

  2. For your phone or tablet, SIGN IN will be behind the dots.

  3. Sometimes, when an advanced service is available to you, a sign-in prompt will appear. Click or tap the sign in icon to login.

  4. If you are a student, instructor or staff member at Southern, use the first option. Everyone else should use the second.

  5. Fill in your SCSU account ID and password.

  6. You should now have access to online full-text articles, materials requests, your library account, your saved searches and all advanced library services.

 Sign In Errors

Sign in problems should be rare. Match your screen to one of the pictures here, and follow our suggestions to handle the issue.

  1. Authentication failed

    If you type in the wrong username or password, you'll get a simple authentication error. Try again. Make sure you're typing your username correctly and that you haven't set CAPS LOCK on your keyboard.

  2. Error page (very rare)

    The library system rejected your sign in request. This has been seen to happen when a library patron has not yet been loaded into the system, or when security settings have been improperly configured on a particular workstation. If necessary, a librarian can create temporary account for you and help sort out the problem.

  3. Something Else

    You've encountered something we don't know about. Contact your local IT Help Desk, and we'll help you get moving again.
